ABOUT US

We believe that the enjoyment of an outing, be it long or short, does not begin when you reach the destination, but begins as soon as you start exploring how and where to go with your loved ones.

Just an hour drive from Pune city, AMRUTRAEE RETREAT is a perfect getaway to enjoy the combined pleasures of urban and rural lifestyle. Layback in the scenic atmosphere and enjoy the times that encourage endless chats with your loved ones.

Enjoy the local cuisine on a dining table with a beautiful view. Spend time reading books in the library right in the middle of the farm. The Nature lovers can spend time watching a wide range of bird species. Experts have certified AMRUTRAEE as a perfect place for sky gazing and photography.

Also, you can go hiking on easy to moderate gradient routes that take you up on the hill to give you a bird eye view of the scenic surroundings.
