Overview

We believe that the enjoyment of an outing, be it long or short, does not begin when you reach the
destination, but begins as soon as you start exploring how and where to go with your loved ones.

Just an hour drive from Pune city, AMRUTRAEE RETREAT is a perfect getaway to enjoy the combined
pleasures of urban and rural lifestyle. Layback in the scenic atmosphere and enjoy the times that
encourage endless chats with your loved ones. Enjoy the local cuisine on a dining table with a beautiful
view. Spend time reading books in the library right in the middle of the farm. The Nature lovers
can spend time watching a wide range of bird species. Experts have certified AMRUTRAEE as a
perfect place for sky gazing and photography. Also, you can go hiking on easy to moderate gradient routes
that take you up on the hill to give you a bird eye view of the scenic surroundings
Sports enthusiasts can enjoy cricket on a full size pitch.
Or game of Tennis on Maharashtra’s first grass court and much more activities to choose from.

Facilities

Rooms

Family Room A


This is a room with 6 beds for larger families or group of friends. It is designed to give excellent cross ventilation, has two washrooms with hot and cold water.

Family Room B


Family room B has four beds. It is designed to keep its roof cool even during hot summer days, has excellent ventilation and an attached washroom with hot and cold water.
