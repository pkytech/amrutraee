Overview

We believe that the enjoyment of an outing, be it long or short, does not begin when you reach the
destination, but begins as soon as you start exploring how and where to go with your loved ones.
Just an hour drive from Pune city, AMRUTRAEE RETREAT is a perfect getaway to enjoy the combined
pleasures of urban and rural lifestyle. Layback in the scenic atmosphere and enjoy the times that
encourage endless chats with your loved ones. Enjoy the local cuisine on a dining table with a beautiful
view. Spend quality time reading books in the library right in the middle of the farm. The Nature lovers
can spend quality time watching a wide range of bird species. Experts have certified AMRUTRAEE as a
perfect place for sky gazing and photography. Also, you can go on hiking easy to moderate gradient routes
that takes you up on the hill to give you a bird eye view of the scenic surroundings and famous Rajgad,
Torna & Sinhgad Forts. Sports enthusiasts can enjoy cricket on a pitch surrounded with net.
Or a game of Tennis on the Maharashtra’s first grass court and a much more activities to choose from.
